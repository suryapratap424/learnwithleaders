About us<\/h2>\n<\/div>

Learn with Leaders is a global education initiative inspiring teenagers to change the way they think so that they can make more fulfilling career and life decisions.<\/span><\/p>\n

Our programs and competitions spanning entrepreneurship, design thinking, leadership, artful thinking, sustainability, MUNs, and finance primarily fill the gap that our traditional education system cannot address.<\/span><\/p>\n

Across our programs, we try to maintain a commonality of students getting to learn new skills, group project work, and international participation.<\/span><\/p>\n

We, Learn with Leaders, are the partner platform of the student clubs and student organizations amplifying the remarkable autonomy and responsibility shouldered by these driven clubs and organizations, promoting the significance and effectiveness of these impactful programs.<\/span><\/p>\n<\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div>
