At Harvard, he\u2019s involved in the competitive Model UN team ICMUN, Harvard\u2019s college conference HNMUN, serves as the Secretary General for HMUN India, and is the Treasurer of the International Relations Council. Arpit likes to remind himself that before he got sucked into Model UN work, he used to enjoy reading, loved biking along the Charles, and was obsessed with his research in the Economics department.<\/div>\n
