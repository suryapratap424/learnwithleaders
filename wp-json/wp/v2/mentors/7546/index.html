Ovul Sezer is a behavioral a scientist and a stand-up comedian who observes life with science, wonder and humor.<\/p>\n

During the day, she is an Assistant Professor of Management and Organizations at Cornell University. Late at night, she performs stand-up routines in comedy clubs. She applies the same observational tools to both jobs: She identifies a particular human behavior that that is odd, interesting, or confusing, and either study it in-depth using experiments or describe it with humor.<\/p>\n

Professor Sezer earned her bachelor\u2019s degree from Harvard University in Applied Mathematics and her PhD from Harvard University in Organizational Behavior.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Awards & Recognitions<\/strong><\/p>\n

Professor Sezer\u2019s dissertation research on impression management won the Harvard Business School Wyss Award for Excellence in Doctoral Research across all departments at Harvard Business School.<\/em><\/p>\n
