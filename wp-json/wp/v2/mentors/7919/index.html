George Benaroya, Vice President, CFO.<\/strong><\/p>\n

Benaroya uses best practices from his career at Procter & Gamble, Tetra Pak, Nivea, and Private Equity (read more). Benaroya spent one-half of his career at headquarters and one-half running local operations in 9 countries (sales of $20 million to $ 2 Billion). He also teaches a graduate class at NYU (New York University) and the University of Salamanca. Students will be able to ask questions and get support from Benaroya himself. None of the sessions are pre-recorded or taught by TAs.<\/p>\n
