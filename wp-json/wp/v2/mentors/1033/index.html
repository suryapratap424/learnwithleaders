“Dr. Omar Antonio Alvarez-Pousa is a passionate intrapreneur in the business environment & an innovative professor in the academic field. For more than 30 years, he has developed expertise at international pharmaceutical companies in the US (Zoetis and Pfizer) and in Argentina (Bayer), in addition to top Argentinean companies in the transportation and construction industries, which allowed him to gain his expertise in organizational design, business models and processes, information technology applied to business, startups development and integration.<\/p>\n

He has boosted academic experience at NYU, in the US, from 2004, at King’s College-IFAPP. from 2018, Johnson & Wales University and Miami Business Technological University form 2019, and previously at several national and private universities in Argentina, for 18 years, becoming a specialist in developing innovative business ideas through business plans and cases translated into degree courses.<\/p>\n

His academic credentials include an Argentinean Doctorate in Administration, plus an MBA and a CPA – member of the Bar in Buenos Aires-, along with Certificates in the US, from NYU, Berkeley, and MIT. Currently, he lives in Key Biscayne dedicating his spare time to research organizational change.”<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
