As a high school student, you’re about to embark on a journey that will take your writing skills to the next level. Research writing assignments are a common part of your classes and they’re more than just boring essays. These assignments are your chance to show off your critical thinking skills, improve your writing abilities, and learn about new and exciting topics.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Here’s what you’ll need to do to ace your research writing assignments:<\/span><\/p>\n

    \n
  • Become a Detective<\/strong>: Researching is the first step to writing a research paper, and it’s a blast! You get to be like Sherlock Holmes, searching for the best sources to help you write your paper. But, be careful! Not all sources are created equal. You’ll need to learn how to identify reliable sources, check their credibility, and combine information from multiple sources to create a solid paper.<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

     <\/p>\n

      \n
    • Think Outside the Box<\/strong>: Research writing requires you to analyze and interpret information, form opinions, and draw conclusions based on evidence. This is where you get to show off your critical thinking skills! You’ll learn how to think beyond what you already know and make informed decisions.<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

       <\/p>\n

        \n
      • Write Like a Pro<\/strong>: Research writing is an opportunity for you to hone your writing abilities. You’ll learn how to write clearly and concisely, and how to present your ideas in a way that’s convincing and engaging.<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

         <\/p>\n

          \n
        • Get Organized<\/strong>: Research papers can get messy, but with the right tools, you’ll be able to keep your thoughts and ideas in order. You’ll learn how to create an outline, write a thesis statement, and organize your paper into neat and tidy paragraphs.<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

           <\/p>\n

            \n
          • Time Management<\/strong>: Research writing takes time and effort, but with proper planning and execution, you’ll be able to get it done in no time. You’ll learn how to manage your time effectively, set goals, and work consistently towards meeting your deadlines.<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

            Do you identify as a high school student who loves to know the “WHY” of things, deep diving into the details and complexities of topics, asking questions, and seeking answers through various sources? Learn with Leaders has just the perfect opportunity for you<\/p>\n

             <\/p>\n
